About Me

Can an office assistant teach themself to program? This portfolio website can be your answer.

I am a Web Developer with working proficiency in HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, jQuery, Node.js, JavaScript, and many other frameworks and libraries (see below). I am also very comfortable working with the Linux command line, using Git version control, and utilizing website content management. Furthermore, I possess a fundamental understanding of mobile-first design concepts, and working with APIs.

After over a year of study, I am ready to contribute to the world in this new way, and I'm looking forward to bringing the best traits of a creative developer to the table. Over my career, I have learned how to get projects done quickly and precisely, handle pressure in important moments, work with different types of people, and take initiative to pick up slack when needed. That, plus my ability to learn quickly has helped me to become a creative but also grounded developer. I hope you enjoy my projects. I would love to work with a team that is truly passionate about what they create and that is striving to consistently move forward. Thank you for taking the time to look at my portfolio!
